About Me

Welcome to my world of sounds! I am Lee Xinyang, the creator behind XYL'sounds. As a student, I have a passion for learning, particularly in science and technology. I enjoy challenging myself academically, engaging in activities, and dedicating time to volunteer work. I have a profound love for sound; music resonates deeply within me, and sound design and recording serve as one of my ways to express emotions. White noice has always been one of my passions, and I believe in the power of sound to inspire, heal, and connect people.

With a background in music composition and a love for experimentation, I strive to create unique and meaningful sonic experiences. Join me on this journey as we explore the beautiful realm of white noice together.
